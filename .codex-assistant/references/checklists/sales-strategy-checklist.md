# Sales Strategy Checklist

- Who is the buyer, champion, user, and blocker?
- What pain is urgent enough to justify a buying motion now?
- What proof points, ROI framing, or risk-reduction signals matter most?
- What objections are likely around price, trust, switching cost, procurement, or implementation?
- What sales motion fits the product stage: founder-led, outbound, inbound, partner-led, or self-serve assisted?
- What is the shortest path from first interest to qualified conversation or closed deal?
- What assets are missing: pitch, case study, one-pager, demo script, pricing narrative, objection handling?
- What should be measured across pipeline, conversion, sales cycle length, and win/loss reasons?
