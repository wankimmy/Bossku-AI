# Paid Acquisition and Monetization Playbook

Use this when the task is to turn attention into revenue using paid channels, pricing, and monetization design.

## Workflow

1. Define the offer, funnel step, audience, country, and business goal.
2. Select the paid channels that fit the stage, budget, and buyer intent.
3. Map campaigns by intent, audience, offer, and landing page.
4. Recommend messaging, keyword or audience structure, creative angles, and budget guardrails.
5. Align CAC expectations with monetization, margins, and payback logic.
6. Propose pricing or packaging decisions that improve conversion and revenue quality.
7. Output a test plan with measurement, stop rules, and iteration loops.

## Output expectation

- paid channel strategy
- campaign structure
- audience and intent targets
- landing page and offer requirements
- budget and CAC logic
- monetization recommendation
- next experiments
