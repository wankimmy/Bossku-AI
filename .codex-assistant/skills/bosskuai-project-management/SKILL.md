# BosskuAI Project Management

Use this skill when the task is about coordinating execution, reducing slippage, clarifying ownership, or creating a working delivery rhythm.

## Workflow

1. Define the desired outcome, owner, deadline, and definition of done.
2. Break work into milestones, slices, and handoffs.
3. Identify blockers, dependencies, and risky assumptions.
4. Recommend operating rhythm, review cadence, and escalation points.
5. Highlight the smallest next action that improves delivery confidence and team clarity.

## Output expectation

- goal and scope
- owners and responsibilities
- milestone or sprint plan
- blockers and risks
- cadence and reporting shape
- recommended next action
