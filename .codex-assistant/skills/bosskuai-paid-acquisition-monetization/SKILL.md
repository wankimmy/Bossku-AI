# BosskuAI Paid Acquisition and Monetization

Use this skill when the user needs a practical plan for paid growth and monetization, including Google Ads and other performance channels.

## Workflow

1. Define the offer, country, audience, budget, and desired conversion action.
2. Select the right paid channels for intent and stage.
3. Build campaign structure by audience, keyword or intent cluster, creative angle, and landing page.
4. Recommend budget logic, CAC guardrails, and stop-loss criteria.
5. Tie acquisition strategy back to pricing, packaging, monetization model, and payback expectations.
6. Recommend the smallest paid tests that can produce useful learning quickly.

## Output expectation

- paid channel strategy
- campaign structure
- keyword or audience plan
- landing page needs
- budget and CAC logic
- monetization recommendation
- next paid experiments
