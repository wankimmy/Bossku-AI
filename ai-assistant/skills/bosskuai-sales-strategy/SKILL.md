# BosskuAI Sales Strategy

Use this skill when the task is about revenue generation, closing deals, shaping the sales motion, or improving conversion from interest to purchase.

## Workflow

1. Identify the buyer, champion, end user, and trigger for purchase.
2. Clarify the product value in business, financial, or operational terms.
3. Select the right motion for the stage: founder-led, outbound, inbound, partner-led, or self-serve assisted.
4. Surface the likely objections, proof gaps, and trust barriers.
5. Recommend messaging, assets, and next sales moves that can improve pipeline quality and close rate.

## Output expectation

- ICP and buyer map
- sales motion
- value and ROI framing
- objections and rebuttals
- needed sales assets
- next pipeline or conversion move

**Deliverables (copy-paste friendly):** ICP + buyer map; objection handling + rebuttals; value/ROI framing; list of needed sales assets; next pipeline or conversion move. Use the sales-strategy checklist and playbook for structure.
