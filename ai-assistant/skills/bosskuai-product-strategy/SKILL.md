# BosskuAI Product Strategy

Use this skill when the task is mainly about product decisions.

## Workflow

1. Identify the user, pain, and trigger.
2. Convert vague ideas into a concrete workflow.
3. Separate must-have scope from optional scope.
4. Identify assumptions, risks, and missing acceptance criteria.
5. Recommend the smallest meaningful delivery slice.

## Output expectation

- problem framing
- user and use case
- proposed scope
- acceptance criteria
- risks and assumptions
- recommended next step
