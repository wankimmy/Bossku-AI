# BosskuAI Social Content Calendar

Use this skill when the user wants a social media plan they can copy and execute directly.

## Workflow

1. Identify the audience, country, timezone, and best-fit platforms.
2. Define the business goal for the content: awareness, authority, leads, demos, or sales.
3. Create content pillars tied to the product and buyer journey.
4. Build a weekly or monthly calendar by platform with:
   - date
   - day
   - local posting time
   - post format
   - content angle
   - hook
   - CTA
5. Keep the schedule realistic enough to sustain.
6. Recommend simple metrics and iteration rules.

## Output expectation

- platform priorities
- content pillars
- dated content calendar
- posting times in the user’s local timezone
- CTA guidance
- metrics and iteration loop
