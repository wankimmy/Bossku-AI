# BosskuAI Marketing and Growth

Use this skill when the task is about getting attention, demand, adoption, or distribution.

## Workflow

1. Define the audience, buyer, and trigger.
2. Clarify the core message and positioning.
3. Select realistic channels for the current stage.
4. Map the path from awareness to activation.
5. Recommend experiments, loops, or campaigns grounded in the product reality.

## Output expectation

- audience
- positioning
- messaging
- channels
- funnel or growth loop
- recommended next experiments
