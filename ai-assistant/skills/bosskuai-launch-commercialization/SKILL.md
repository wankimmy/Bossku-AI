# BosskuAI Launch Commercialization

Use this skill when the real question is: "Can we launch this product successfully, make it attractive enough to read and buy, and learn our way to product-market fit?"

## Workflow

1. Assess whether the product is launchable yet from an engineering, UX, trust, and operational perspective.
2. Define the target market, country, ICP, buyer, user, and trigger for purchase.
3. Clarify the positioning, value proposition, and differentiation story.
4. Evaluate SEO and GEO readiness:
   - search intent
   - landing page clarity
   - content structure
   - answer-first copy
   - proof and trust signals
5. Build the marketing plan:
   - audience
   - messaging
   - channels
   - campaigns
   - funnel assumptions
6. Build the sales and monetization strategy:
   - sales motion
   - pricing and packaging
   - objection handling
   - region-specific considerations
7. Define what product-market-fit evidence should be measured next.
8. Recommend the smallest launch plan that can validate demand without overbuilding.

## Output expectation

- launch readiness assessment
- target market and country strategy
- positioning and conversion narrative
- SEO/GEO recommendations
- marketing plan
- sales strategy
- monetization recommendation
- PMF signals and launch risks
- next launch plan
