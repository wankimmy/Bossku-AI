# Project Management Checklist

- What is the outcome, owner, deadline, and definition of done?
- What work is in scope now versus explicitly out of scope?
- What are the phases, milestones, and dependencies?
- What is blocked, risky, irreversible, or likely to slip?
- What must be validated before the next tranche of work begins?
- Who needs to be informed, consulted, or accountable?
- What is the review cadence: daily, weekly, milestone-based, or launch-based?
- What is the smallest next step that unblocks progress?
- What signals show the project is off track early?
