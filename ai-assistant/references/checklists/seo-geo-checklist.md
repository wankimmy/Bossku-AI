# SEO and GEO Checklist

- What search intent or question is the content trying to satisfy?
- What exact audience wording is being targeted?
- Is the information architecture clear enough for search and answer engines?
- Does the content answer the question directly before adding detail?
- Are entities, topics, and relationships explicit?
- Is the content structured for snippets, summaries, and citation-style extraction?
- Are originality, evidence, and trust signals strong enough to stand out?
- Is the page or asset useful even without ranking?
